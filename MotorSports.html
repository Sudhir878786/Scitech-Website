ABOUT US

IIT Bhilai Motorsports club was founded in 2017 by a group of enthusiastic students of IIT Bhilai. The club's main purpose is to spread automotive culture in the institute. Today it has grown to its strength since its foundation due to the team, fully filled with passion and desire. Currently the team has 40+ members and comprises of 25 core members. Our team is all set to participate in eBAJA 2023.
